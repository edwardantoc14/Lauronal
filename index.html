OUR STORY

Our passion for Philippine delicacies began with family traditions and cherished memories around homemade kakanin and festive gatherings. Inspired by these flavors of home, we created our brand to share authentic, freshly made Filipino treats with everyone.

Each delicacy is prepared with care, using time-honored recipes that celebrate our rich culture and heritage. From our kitchen to your table, we proudly bring you the truetaste of the Philippines.
